NUTRITION BASICS

Understanding the basics of nutrition is essential for achieving your fitness goals. Here's a brief overview:

Macronutrients

Macronutrients are the nutrients that provide energy and are required in large amounts by the body. They include:

  • Carbohydrates: The body's primary source of energy. Found in foods like grains, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Proteins: Important for building and repairing tissues, as well as supporting immune function. Found in foods like meat, fish, eggs, and legumes.
  • Fats: Provide energy, support cell growth, and help absorb certain vitamins. Found in foods like oils, nuts, and avocados.

Micronutrients

Micronutrients are essential vitamins and minerals that are required in smaller amounts by the body. They play crucial roles in various bodily functions, including metabolism, immune function, and bone health. Common micronutrients include:

  • Vitamins: Organic compounds that help regulate bodily processes and support overall health. Examples include vitamin C, vitamin D, and the B vitamins.
  • Minerals: Inorganic elements that are necessary for various physiological functions. Examples include calcium, iron, magnesium, and zinc.

By understanding the roles of macronutrients and micronutrients in the body, you can make informed dietary choices to support your fitness goals and overall well-being.
